WHO WE ARE

The Digital Inclusion Project is a strategic educational initiative designed to bridge the gap between emerging technologies, local communities and students. Jointly implemented by Althash University California, Plateau State Polytechnic, and Web3.0 Alliance Ltd, the project empowers learners with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving digital economy.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Democratize Tech Access

Promote Academic-Industry Collaboration

Build Local Capacity

Foster Innovation

At the Digital Inclusion Hub, people receive personalized, one-on-one tech support that empowers them to navigate the digital world and access essential community services.
