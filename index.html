About Us

Welcome to the Web3 Alliance, a global coalition of seasoned Web3 and blockchain experts with a collective experience exceeding 8 years. Committed to collaboration, research, and development, we champion the advancement of blockchain technology, decentralized applications (DApps), smart contracts, and other innovative solutions. Our mission is to propel the adoption of Web3 technologies, fostering an open and interconnected digital landscape. With a vision for a future where decentralized technologies drive inclusivity, security, and connectivity, the Web3 Alliance pioneers the evolution of the decentralized web. Join us in shaping a digital environment that transcends boundaries, creating opportunities for widespread adoption and positive impact across industries.

Mission

The Web3 Alliance is committed to fostering collaboration, research, and development within the Web3 ecosystem. Our focus includes promoting blockchain technology, decentralized applications (DApps), smart contracts, and other innovative solutions.

Our mission is to drive the adoption of Web3 technologies while empowering a more open, secure, and interconnected digital landscape.

Vision

We envision a future where decentralized technologies play a central role in shaping a more inclusive, transparent, and resilient digital economy.

Through collaboration and innovation, Web3 Alliance strives to advance the decentralized web, create opportunities for widespread adoption, and drive positive impact across industries worldwide.

Digital Inclusion Project

A Collaboration between Althash University California, Plateau State Polytechnic & Web3.0 Alliance Ltd

The Digital Inclusion Project is a strategic educational initiative designed to bridge the gap between emerging technologies, local communities and students. Jointly implemented by Althash University California, Plateau State Polytechnic, and Web3.0 Alliance Ltd, the project empowers learners with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving digital economy.

Our Mission

To make advanced digital education—covering blockchain, artificial intelligence, Web3, and other emerging technologies—accessible to individuals in Plateau State and beyond, regardless of socioeconomic background.

Objectives

  1. Democratize Access to Technology – Ensure that students and community members, including those in underserved areas, can access quality tech education and digital tools.
  2. Build Local Capacity – Train participants in in-demand digital skills, enabling them to compete globally while solving local problems.
  3. Foster Innovation & Entrepreneurship – Equip learners to create technology-driven solutions that stimulate economic growth and social development.
  4. Promote Academic-Industry Collaboration – Connect learners to real-world projects, mentors, and industry networks to enhance career readiness.

Key Features

  • Hybrid Learning Model – Online + on-campus flexibility.
  • Globally Recognized Curriculum – Covering blockchain, fintech, AI, and related fields.
  • Practical, Project-Based Training – Work on real-world problems and portfolio-ready projects.
  • Entrepreneurship Activation – Courses and mentorship to encourage tech-based startups.
  • Community Outreach – Free slots for grassroots learners with potential.

Expected Impact

  • Digital Skills for All – Equip hundreds yearly with future-ready skills.
  • Economic Empowerment – Create access to jobs, internships, and entrepreneurial opportunities.
  • Innovation Ecosystem – Position Plateau State as a hub for technology & innovation.
  • Startup Generation – Inspire student & community-led startups for job creation and resilience.

Through the Digital Inclusion Project, we are not only educating the next generation of tech leaders but also igniting the entrepreneurial spirit needed to transform ideas into impactful ventures—ensuring no community is left behind in the digital revolution.
